Bricks Coggin is the owner of A Bricks Coggin CPA, PLLC and serves as associate partner who works directly with our clients in every aspect of our CPA services. Bricks has over 13 years’ auditing experience with specialism in non-profit organizations, government, single audits, and manufacturing and construction industries. Notable organizations and agencies where Bricks has taken part include AICPA and the TXCPA.

Now a trusted Texas CPA for over 6 years, Bricks offers his extensive experience to the needs of businesses and individuals through a boutique firm that organizes CPA services specifically around the client. With A Bricks Coggin CPA, PLLC, Bricks has the pleasure of meeting with clients and discussing their various financial and business goals while creating personalized strategies just for them. Before founding his own firm, Bricks believed that high-touch CPA services were getting harder and harder to come by for businesses, and that a one-size-fits-all approach actually created disadvantages for businesses on both sides of the scale. That is why he is never too eager to outgrow his firm’s primary purpose. It turns out that’s exactly what people always need.

"For me, coming to work every day is never just about the numbers — it’s about building bonds with the very community I live in."

Born in Mobile, Alabama, Bricks moved to Texas at the early age of three, but still counts himself as a Very Proud Texan! Bricks enjoys his Texas lifestyle full of family, pets, and the great outdoors. He has four Goldendoodle dogs, named Hono, Lulu, Kiki, and Waialae, and loves to sail, row, and just about anything that gets him out on the water.
