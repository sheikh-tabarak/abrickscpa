Bricks Coggin

Owner & Associate Partner

Bricks Coggin is the owner of A Bricks Coggin CPA, PLLC and serves as associate partner who works directly with our clients in every aspect of our CPA services. With 13+ years’ auditing experience across nonprofits, government, and industries, Bricks brings unmatched expertise and a boutique, client-first approach.

Now a trusted Texas CPA for over 6 years, Bricks meets with clients personally to create strategies tailored to their unique financial and business goals. Born in Alabama and raised in Texas, he’s a proud Texan who enjoys life with family, his four Goldendoodles, and outdoor adventures on the water.
