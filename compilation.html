Compilation Process

We approach each compilation with diligence, reviewing your financial data and requesting supporting documents to produce the most comprehensive statement possible.

Thanks to our industry knowledge, we evaluate your reporting policies and fairly present all available data. Although a compilation does not include internal control analysis or verification of records, it often meets the requirements of bank lending covenants and stakeholder interests.
