About A Bricks Coggin CPA, PLLC

Delivering personalized, tech-powered CPA services that put client needs first — since 2017.

A Bricks Coggin CPA, PLLC is a Texas Certified Public Accounting firm based in Southlake, Texas. Since being founded in 2017, we have proudly met our goal of providing personalized attention to the accounting needs of businesses and individuals in the South, and have since expanded to offer the same focused customer service to clients nationwide.

Even as we continue to grow, we never outpace the individual needs of our clients, which we consider the true cornerstone of the industry and the reason we cherish our reputation as the local business CPA of choice.

In line with our attentive solutions, our founding partner and senior associates deliver comprehensive CPA auditing services tailored to Non-Profit businesses — a sector currently observing major changes in tax guidelines and updated reporting requirements.

With A Bricks Coggin CPA, PLLC, Non-Profit associations, organizations and foundations have the means to navigate these updated requirements without interruption while taking full advantage of the latest suite of benefits. Through our Single Audit services, we provide a clear path towards compliance, transparency, and effective budgeting — ensuring maximum funding opportunities.

Our clients are positioned at the forefront of tax and regulatory updates, avoiding non-compliance penalties, exemption revocations, and other setbacks — while being fully prepared for upcoming policy changes.

We consider it our duty to equip businesses with the knowledge to bolster operations through financial awareness and resilience. For organizations without large in-house accounting teams, our Audit Services fill that critical gap with expertise and confidence.

Over the years, we have stayed in lockstep with emerging technologies that continue to transform the accounting landscape. By deploying cutting-edge software and methodologies, we deliver cost-savings, efficiency, and accuracy that empower businesses through smarter CPA services.

Every engagement is tailored to the specific goals of our clients’ industries, with tools designed to automate and process data into clear and actionable answers. Our hands-on approach, backed by technology, ensures we reveal the perfect solution for your needs.
